ABOUT OMU SOCCER CLUB

OMU Soccer Club (est. 2015) is one of the premier youth developmental clubs in the tri-state area. We are a founding member of MLS NEXT, the highest level of youth soccer in the United States. We have had success on several levels in our relatively short existence, while also being able to place of youth players at various colleges and professional clubs.

OMU Soccer Club provides a total player pathway model for each player's respective ambitions, be it youth, high school, college or professional. Specific syllabi are developed to assist players at different stages of their development. Our main objective is to create a culture of "excellence" through constant and consistent improvement.
